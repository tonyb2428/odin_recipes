Salmon

Description

A Salmon recipes for the Pescatarians out there

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Blackening Spices: Paprica salt garlic powder, onion power, cayenne, thyme, oregano
  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter
  • Lemon

Directions

  1. Pat salmon dry
  2. Brush with butter
  3. start the salmon fillets flesh- side down in a hot pan. Flip each piece over and cook on the other side
  4. Top with lemon juice
