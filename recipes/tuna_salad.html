Tuna Salad

Description

A Tuna Salad recipes for the Pescatarians out there

Ingredients

  • Canned Tuna
  • Mayonnaise
  • Celery
  • Red Onion
  • Mustard
  • Salt and Peper
  • Parsley

Directions

  1. Drain Tuna
  2. Add the mayonnaise, celery, red onion, parsley, and dijon mustard
  3. Use a fork to mix everything together while breaking up any large pieces of tuna
  4. Season with and pepper
